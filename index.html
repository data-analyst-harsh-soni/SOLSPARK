Help Center

Here you will find answers to frequently asked questions about solar energy, our calculator, and services.

  • What is solar energy?

    Solar energy is energy generated by converting sunlight into electricity, typically using photovoltaic (PV) panels.

  • What are the benefits of solar energy?

    Solar energy reduces electricity bills, decreases the carbon footprint, and provides energy independence.

  • How often should I clean the solar panels?

    Panels should be cleaned every 15-30 days, especially during dry, dusty seasons, to prevent a 15-20% drop in efficiency.

  • What is the Payback Period for a typical system?

    The typical payback period ranges from 4 to 6 years, depending on the system size, upfront cost, and local electricity tariffs.

  • Do solar systems require a separate Battery?

    Grid-tied systems (On-Grid) usually do not need a battery. Only Off-Grid or Hybrid systems use batteries for nighttime backup power.
